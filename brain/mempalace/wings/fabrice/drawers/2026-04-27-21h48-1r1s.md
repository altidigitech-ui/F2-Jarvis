The exercise hits different when you actually run it. Most merchants expect 1-2 surprises. What they find is closer to 5-6, and at least a couple come from apps they thought they'd removed months ago.

Uninstalling an app and canceling its billing are two separate systems on Shopify. The charge just keeps running with no alert, nothing visible in the app dashboard because the app isn't even there anymore.

I've scanned 90+ stores and ghost billing from removed apps shows up in around 85% of them. The amounts are small individually — $12, $19, $29 — but over 6-12 months it compounds fast.

**Comment 2 — Matthew Hapgood**

This is by design, not a bug. The app install and the billing subscription are two separate records on Shopify's side. Uninstalling removes one. It doesn't touch the other.

Across 90+ store scans, the average ghost billing window after app removal is 4-6 weeks. Some merchants are months in before they notice. The cancellation step is buried in the app's own settings page — which is only accessible before you uninstall, not after.
